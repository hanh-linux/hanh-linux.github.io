Hanh Linux

What exactly is this?

Hanh Linux is an open-source, assessible and reliable operating system. Provide the ability to hand on the most cutting-edge softwares through the power of Arch Linux.



Purpose:

  • Install anything: Hanh Linux just contains the base package, this mean you can install and configure softwares - in any ways you want. No restrictions. Your system, your control!


  • Lightweight: System size on disk below 2.25GB and ISO file below 600MB, that's really impressed. But wait, what's more? Thanks to Artix, you can have a life without using a bloated and insecure init daemon by using OpenRC - Gentoo universal init daemon.


  • Free: Hanh Linux is completely free. We created it for you, you are the user. You are our focus, not money. Our team do this as our passion. Free is better, right?
