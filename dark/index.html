Hanh Linux

What exactly is this?

Hanh Linux is an independent, source-based Linux distribution.
Hanh Linux is fast, lightweight, simple and flexible.



Goals

  • Fast and lightweight: All packages are compiled with -O3 and we use implementations of packages that they are lighter and faster than the original.


  • Simple: If anything can be simple, let's just simplify it. Our implementations should be written from shell and C/C++, and we love usable and simple package implementations, too.


  • Flexible: Build packages with flavor, build with your modifications.


  • systemd-less: We use OpenRC as the init system. Non-systemd, simple.


  • POSIX compatible: We want our projects can be easily portable to other operating systems.
